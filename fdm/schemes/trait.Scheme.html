Scheme

The scheme currently will only support solving 1D hyperbolic equation.

$$ u_t + f(u)_x = 0 $$

The explicit scheme works like $$ u_{j}^{n+1} = u_{j}^{n} - \frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} (h_{j+}^{n} - h_{j-}^{n}), $$

where $h_{j+}^{n}, h_{j-}^{n}$ are the numerical flux.

Spreading speed

$$ v_{j+} = \begin{cases} \frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} \frac{f_{j+1} - f_{j}}{u_{j+1}-u_{j}} ,& u_{j} != u_{j+1} \\ \frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} f^{'}(u_j),& u_{j} = u_{j+1} \end{cases} $$

$$ v_{j-} = \begin{cases} \frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} \frac{f_{j} - f_{j-1}}{u_{j}-u_{j-1}} ,& u_{j} != u_{j-1} \\ \frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} f^{'}(u_{j-1}),& u_{j} = u_{j-1} \end{cases} $$

The return size = n + ext
